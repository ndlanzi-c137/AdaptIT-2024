Home Water Purification Recipes

  • Boiling: Boil water for at least 1-3 minutes to kill bacteria, viruses, and parasites.
  • Bleach Disinfection: Add 2 drops of unscented household bleach to 1 liter of water. Let it sit for 30 minutes.
  • UV Purification: Expose water to direct sunlight for 6 hours using a clear plastic bottle to kill bacteria and viruses.
  • Filtration: Use a home water filter to remove contaminants like heavy metals, chlorine, and bacteria.
